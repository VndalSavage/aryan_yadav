120 mins

In terms of serious matters, including crime coverage and the opinions surrounding it, there is absolutely no exception. This podcast is a discussion between students who are very aware of the media and how it works and how the use of social media has had an effect on well-known crimes of the past. It showcases just how much this trend can continue to affect the motions and outcomes of crimes in the future.
